    • About

    From playing guitar at an early Age, Chris has been immersed in music for the majority of his life. From metal to rock to EDM to Jazz. Chris has dipped his fingers and experimented in any genres.

    Although still studying Audio Production at Collarts, Chris has interned at Studios such as Homesurgery based in Brunswick. During the time of which had been apart of the recording of Albums from Bakers eddy, Planet of the 8s and Kingswood to name a few.

    Always looking to experiment, every recording comes out with a little extra spice to set it apart from the rest.
